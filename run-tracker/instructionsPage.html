Welcome to the Instructions page for Run Tracker!

1. Click on the top left menu to select what you want to do.
2. To start a run, select 'Home' and tap the START button! Your run will start
recording and your route will be shown on the screen.
3. To stop recording your run, tap the STOP button. You will be asked to name your saved route.
4. To view your saved routes, select 'Previous Runs' from the menu. All your saved routes will be shown there!
5. On the 'Previous Runs' page, you can choose to view more details about your routes or delete your routes.

Have fun using Run Tracker!
